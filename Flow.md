Flows are used to form [topologies](Topology) where systems of connections are setup. 
 
3 types of dataflow:
* Simplex
	* single direction flow
* Half--duplex
	* both direction flow one at a time (cannot be at same time)
* Full-duplex
	* Both direction flow at the same time
